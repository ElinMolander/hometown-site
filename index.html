Top three things to enjoy in Göteborg

Majorna

Cultural hotspot with a vibrant restaurant scene. Visit exhibitions and go to nice resturants.

The Archipelago

The Gothenburg archipelago is amazing and there are so many islands for you to discover.

Craft beer

Stigbergets, Poppels, All in Brewing or Dugges? this is just a short list off the many breweries in Gothenburg.
